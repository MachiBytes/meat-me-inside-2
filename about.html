About

MEAT ME INSIDE

Meat Me Inside has earned its reputation as one of the finest steakhouses in Manila, delivering an unparalleled dining experience with every cut. With twenty-one locations worldwide, we take pride in our signature USDA Prime Black Angus steaks, fresh seafood, and an award-winning wine selection, bringing together the perfect harmony of flavor and craftsmanship.


Our beef is carefully hand-selected for its superior marbling, texture, and flavor, then dry-aged to perfection to intensify its richness. Each cut—whether it's our signature Porterhouse, Prime New York Strip, Ribeye, or Filet Mignon—is expertly carved and grilled to sizzling perfection, locking in its mouthwatering juiciness with a beautifully caramelized crust.


Beyond our legendary steaks, Meat Me Inside offers an exquisite selection of seafood, including fresh sashimi-grade tuna, succulent Maine lobsters, and delicate Norwegian salmon. Our classic sides, from creamy spinach to golden-brown roasted potatoes, perfectly complement any dish, while appetizers such as plump oysters, sizzling bacon, and vibrant salads set the stage for an unforgettable meal.


At Meat Me Inside, every bite is an experience, and every meal is a celebration of taste, quality, and passion.
